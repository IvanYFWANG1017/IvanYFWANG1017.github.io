Introduction to Ansteel Roll Co. Ltd.

Ansteel Roll Co. Ltd., established in 1936, is located in Anshan, Liaoning. With 85 years of expertise, it is one of China’s leading roll manufacturers. The company specializes in advanced roll production for the steel industry, certified with ISO9001, and offers high-quality products for hot and cold rolling mills.

Main Products

  • High Speed Steel Roll
  • High Chrome Steel Roll
  • Enhanced High NiCrMo ICDP Roll
  • High Nickel-Niobium Wear-resistant Roll
  • Other: Semi-steel, Cast/Forged Steel Rolls
