Introduction to Ansteel Roll Co. Ltd.

Ansteel Roll Co. Ltd., established in 1936, is located in Anshan, Liaoning, with a history of 85 years in roll manufacturing. It is one of the largest specialized roll manufacturers in China. The company is known for its extensive experience, advanced technology, and ISO9001 certification. Their primary products are plate and strip rolls used in rolling mills.

Main Products

  • High Speed Steel Roll
  • High Chrome Steel Roll
  • Enhanced High NiCrMo ICDP Roll
  • High Nickel-Niobium Wear-resistant Roll
  • Other: Semi-steel, Cast/Forged Steel Rolls
