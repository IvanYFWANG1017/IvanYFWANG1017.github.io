Company Introduction

ANGANG (ASIA-PACIFIC) TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD. was established in September 2021 and is an experienced and innovative professional integrated management company for grinding rolls. Our main business focuses on collaborating with steel companies in the Asia Pacific region, including collaboration with companies such as Yieh United Steel Corporation, Ltd. and YIEH PHUI ENTERPRISE CO., LTD. based on Taiwan.
