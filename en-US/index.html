Welcome to ANGANG (ASIA-PACIFIC) TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD.

Company Overview：
ANGANG (ASIA-PACIFIC) TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD. is a Singapore-registered company founded in 2021. Its mission is to provide high-quality metallurgical rolls, large industrial grinders, special smelting equipment, and essential spare parts for steel mills across the Asia-Pacific region.

Industry Experience：
One of the founders, Mr. Wang, entered the steel industry in 1997 and worked at POSCO in South Korea from 1997 to 2006, where he gained extensive experience. Understanding the diverse needs of steel mills, he established several companies in China, building a strong reputation over the years. He has formed strategic partnerships with Ansteel Heavy Machinery Co., Ltd., Ansteel Roll Co., Ltd., and Guiyang Xianfeng Machine Tool Co., Ltd. Additionally, the company’s major clients include Shagang Group, POSCO, BlueScope (China and Vietnam), YUSCO (Yieh United Group and Yieh Phui Enterprise Co., Ltd.), and Ansteel Guangzhou Lianzhong.

Partnerships and Agency Rights：
ANGANG (ASIA-PACIFIC) holds agency rights for equipment from Ansteel Roll Co., Ltd., Guiyang Xianfeng Machine Tool Co., Ltd., Netherlands LISMAR, and Suzhou Shining Sun Technology Co., Ltd., aiming to expand its services to meet the evolving needs of the steel industry.
