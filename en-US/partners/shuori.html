Introduction to Shining Sun

Suzhou Shining Sun Technology Co., Ltd., established in 2023, focuses on the independent development of high-temperature alloy smelting equipment. The company has attracted key sales, technical, and production teams from Suzhou Zhenwu Electric Furnace Co., Ltd., alongside long-term strategic investment from Jiangsu Dongfang Sitong Technology Co., Ltd. It is dedicated to enhancing the control systems and overall competitiveness of smelting equipment. The goal is to grow sales revenue from 50 million RMB to 300-500 million RMB within five years.

Suzhou Zhenwu Electric Furnace Co., Ltd., founded in August 1988, is a national key high-tech enterprise specializing in the development and manufacturing of electromagnetic metallurgy technology and equipment. The company has a strong technical team with expertise in power electronics, mechanical engineering, casting, metallurgy, automation, and software. Collaborating with research institutes and universities, Zhenwu keeps pace with international advancements, focusing on developing technologies and equipment for the casting, metallurgy, and heat treatment industries. Its products are widely used, especially in induction furnaces, where the company holds a leading position both domestically and internationally.

Jiangsu Dongfang Sitong Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2001, is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the development and production of reliable, efficient industrial control power supplies. With over 20 years of experience, the company excels in crystal growth power supplies, voltage regulation, and medium/high-frequency induction power systems. Driven by technological innovation, it has been recognized as a national high-tech enterprise, provincial technology center, and a "Little Giant" specialized enterprise. The company has received multiple certifications (NRTL, CE, ISO9001) and awards, including the National Torch Program and various innovation accolades.

For more information, please visit the official website of Suzhou Shining Sun Technology Co., Ltd.
