Introduction to Suzhou Shining Sun Technology Co., Ltd.

Suzhou Shining Sun Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in the core design technology of control power supplies, which includes:
1.High-Power Vacuum Furnace Smelting Power Supplies: Capable of reaching up to 5000KW, along with temperature control power supplies for vacuum precision casting furnaces.
2.Single-Phase and Three-Phase Vacuum Smelting Stirring Power Supplies.
3.Thyristor Series Resonant Power Supplies: Capable of designing systems with a maximum power of 20,000KW.
4.Thyristor Parallel Resonant Power Supplies: With a design capacity of up to 50,000KW.
5.IGBT Series Resonant Power Supplies: Capable of designing up to 12,000KW.

The company excels in special smelting equipment technology, including vacuum induction furnaces, electroslag remelting furnaces, and vacuum arc remelting furnaces, providing customized core design solutions for clients.
Vacuum Induction Furnace Series:
1.Vacuum Induction Melting Furnace(VIM) & Vacuum Induction Degassing and Pouring Furnace(VIDP)
2.Vacuum Induction Casting Furnace(VIC) & Vacuum Precision Investment Casting Furnace(VPIC)
3.Vacuum CAP Furnace(VCAP)

Electroslag Remelting Furnace Series:
1.Positive Pressure Electro Slag Remelting Furnace (PESR)
2.Vacuum Electro Slag Remelting Furnace (VESR)
3.Electro Slag Remelting Furnace (ESR)

Vacuum Arc Remelting Furnace(VAR): Used for smelting titanium and steel.

The company is committed to continuous innovation, focusing on providing new specialized smelting equipment for industries dealing with precision alloys, high-temperature alloys, corrosion-resistant alloys, and special stainless steel materials. Suzhou Shining Sun Technology Co., Ltd.'s products are widely applied in sectors such as aerospace, power generation, petrochemicals, medical devices, and the automotive industry. Leveraging advanced technology and refined manufacturing processes, the company strives to be a comprehensive supplier of specialized smelting solutions, integrating R&D, manufacturing, and sales, while delivering high-quality products and services to clients across various industries.
