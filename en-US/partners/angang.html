Introduction to Ansteel

Ansteel Roll Co., Ltd., established in 1935, is China's first specialized roll manufacturing enterprise and is part of the Ansteel Group. Located in Anshan, Liaoning Province, the company boasts state-of-the-art casting, machining technology, and advanced production equipment. In recent years, Ansteel Roll has focused on technological innovation and product development, solidifying its position as a leading authority in the domestic roll manufacturing industry.
Products
Ansteel Roll Co., Ltd. primarily produces various types of cast iron and cast steel rolls, with an annual production capacity of 30,000 tons. The products are widely used in both hot and cold rolling steel production, specifically including:

  • High Nickel-Chromium Rolls: Designed for high-strength rolling environments, these rolls provide excellent durability and performance under demanding conditions.
  • High Chromium Steel Rolls: These rolls are characterized by their excellent wear resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and longevity under heavy-duty rolling conditions.
  • Other Rolls: This category includes various types of rolls such as semi-steel rolls, which are designed for specific industrial applications. These rolls offer a balance of toughness and wear resistance, making them suitable for diverse rolling conditions.

Technical Strength
Ansteel Roll Co., Ltd. strictly adheres to the ISO9001 quality management system throughout its production process, ensuring the reliability of its products. In addition to enhancing production capabilities, the company continually introduces advanced technology and equipment to maintain a competitive edge in the market.
Market Position
As a leader in China's roll manufacturing industry, Ansteel Roll Co., Ltd. has built a strong reputation both domestically and internationally. Its products are exported to multiple countries and regions, reflecting the company's commitment to quality and innovation. Through continuous technological advancements and strategic market expansion, Ansteel Roll aims to capture a larger share of the global steel industry, strengthening its presence and competitiveness on an international scale.
