Introduction to ZhangJiagang ShaGongDongLi Mechanical&Electrical Equipment Manufacturing Co., LTD.

Company Overview


ZhangJiagang ShaGongDongLi Mechanical&Electrical Equipment Manufacturing Co., LTD. (hereinafter referred to as "ShaGongDongLi") was established on June 18, 2007, with its headquarters located in Zhangjiagang, Suzhou, Jiangsu Province, within the campus of Shazhou Professional Institute of Technology. The company specializes in the processing of mechanical spare parts, as well as the development, production, and on-site installation of complete mechanical equipment. Since its establishment, ShaGongDongLi has been committed to continuous innovation, aiming to provide high-quality and highly efficient manufacturing solutions for mechanical equipment to its customers.

Quality and Management Systems


The company places a high priority on quality management and continuous improvement, striving to ensure high product quality through a comprehensive management system.
  • October 27, 2008: Achieved ISO9001:2008 Quality Management System Certification
  • May 2010: Passed certification assessment by Japan Suido Corporation
  • December 2021: Certified in the Integration of Informatization and Industrialization Management System
  • December 2023: Recognized as a High-Tech Enterprise


Strategic Partners


On February 20, 2023, ShaGongDongLi signed a strategic partnership agreement withAnsteel Heavy Machinery Co., Ltd.(hereinafter referred to as Ansteel Heavy Machinery). A subsidiary of the Ansteel Group Corporation, Ansteel Heavy Machinery is one of the largest enterprises in China’s metallurgical equipment manufacturing industry. With a history spanning 90 years, Ansteel Heavy Machinery is a high-tech enterprise that designs and manufactures a wide range of metallurgical equipment and spare parts. For more details, refer to Ansteel Heavy Machinery’s Introduction and the Strategic Partnership Agreement between ShaGongDongLi and Ansteel Heavy Machinery.
